**REFERRAL PARTNER AGREEMENT**

This Referral Partner Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] between:

**AO AI Solutions** ("Company"), a business based in Washington, DC metro area, and

**[PARTNER NAME]** ("Partner"), a business based in [PARTNER LOCATION].

---

## 1. REFERRAL PROGRAM

Partner agrees to refer potential clients to Company for AI-powered business systems services, including but not limited to: AI websites, chatbots, lead automation, and CRM integration.

## 2. REFERRAL FEE

Company agrees to pay Partner a referral fee of **15% of the first project invoice amount** for each qualified client referred by Partner who signs a contract and makes initial payment.

- **Payment timing:** Within 30 days of receipt of client's initial payment to Company
- **Minimum threshold:** Referral fees under $100 will be rolled to the following month
- **Payment method:** Bank transfer or check to Partner's account on file

## 3. QUALIFIED REFERRAL

A "Qualified Referral" is a new client who:
- Was not previously in Company's database or pipeline
- Was introduced to Company by Partner (via email introduction, phone, or in-person)
- Signs a service agreement and makes initial payment within 90 days of introduction

## 4. EXCLUSIVITY

This Agreement is **non-exclusive**. Partner may refer clients to other service providers. Company may accept referrals from other partners.

## 5. RECIPROCAL REFERRALS

Company agrees to refer clients to Partner for services within Partner's expertise, as mutually agreed. No referral fee is required from Partner for Company's referrals.

## 6. CO-MARKETING

Company will feature Partner on its website (aoaisolutions.dev) as a recommended partner, subject to mutual approval of listing content.

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep client information confidential and not to solicit each other's clients directly during the term of this Agreement.

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement begins on the date signed and continues until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Referral fees earned prior to termination remain payable.

## 9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Company's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total referral fees paid to Partner in the preceding 12 months.
