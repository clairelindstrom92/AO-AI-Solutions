## FORMATTING RULES FOR ALL POSTS

1. **First line = hook** — must be strong enough to stop the scroll
2. **Max 3–4 lines visible before "see more"** — front-load the value
3. **No more than 5 short paragraphs** — LinkedIn isn't a blog
4. **1 CTA per post, at the end** — not multiple asks
5. **No hashtag spam** — max 3 hashtags, put them at the very end
6. **Recommended hashtags:** #AI #WashingtonDC #BusinessAutomation (rotate based on topic)
7. **Images:** Always better than no image. Use dark navy/cyan brand colors.
8. **Best posting times:** 8–9am EST, Tuesday–Thursday performs best
