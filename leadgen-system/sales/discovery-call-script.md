# Discovery Call Script — AO AI Solutions
**LRP Method: Listen → Repeat → Poke | 30-minute structure**

---

## BEFORE THE CALL (5-min prep)
- [ ] Review contact's LinkedIn profile and company website
- [ ] Note their industry, company size, and any recent news/posts
- [ ] Look up their Google Business profile — reviews, hours, booking system
- [ ] Have the AI Audit template open and ready to take notes
- [ ] Have Calendly link ready for next step close

---

## OPENING (Minutes 0–2) — Rapport + Agenda

"Hey [First Name], thanks for taking the time — I know [Day] mornings can be busy. How are you doing?"

*[Brief rapport — 30 seconds max. Don't fake it. One genuine comment.]*

"Great. So the goal for today is pretty simple — I want to understand what's happening in your business right now, and at the end I'll tell you honestly whether AI automation would actually help you or not. If it's not the right fit, I'll say so. Sound good?"

*[Wait for agreement.]*

"Okay, let me start by asking you a few questions about how your business currently runs, and then I'll share some things we've seen work in your industry. Does that work?"

---

## DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (Minutes 2–17) — Listen Mode

**Goal:** Get them talking. Ask, then shut up. Take notes.

### Current Process
1. "Walk me through what happens when a new potential client or patient contacts your business right now — from the first touch to the first appointment or meeting. What does that look like?"

2. "What happens after hours or on weekends when someone reaches out?"

3. "How many new inquiries or leads would you say come in per week? Ballpark is fine."

### Pain Points
4. "Of those inquiries, roughly what percentage do you think you're actually converting into paying clients or appointments?"

5. "What happens to the ones who don't convert? Do you have any follow-up process?"

6. "Where does it break down most often — is it speed of response, follow-up, or something else?"

### Time & Resources
7. "How much time per week would you estimate your team spends on intake, scheduling, or client follow-up tasks?"

8. "If I could hand back half that time — what would your team do with it?"

### Goals & Timeline
9. "What would a win look like for you in the next 90 days? If we worked together and it went perfectly, what would be different?"

10. "Is this something you're trying to solve in the next 30 days, or more of a 'nice to have someday' thing?"

---

## PAIN AMPLIFICATION (Minutes 17–22) — Quantify the Cost

*Use their own numbers. Don't inflate — understate and let the math speak.*

"Let me make sure I have this right. You said you get roughly [X] inquiries per week, and you're converting about [Y]%. That means you're probably converting [Z] new clients per month."

"Now — if your current response time means you're losing even 10–15% of those inquiries to slower follow-up... at [average value per client], that's roughly [$X] per month in missed revenue. Does that math sound right to you?"

*[Wait. Let the number land.]*

"That's not a failure on your team's part — that's just a gap between what the business needs and what any human team can realistically do at 11pm. That's exactly what automation handles."

---

## SOLUTION BRIDGE (Minutes 22–27) — Transition Without Pitching

"Based on everything you've shared, there are a few things I'd want to look at specifically for [Business Name]."

*[Name 2–3 specific automation opportunities, not generic — tie them to what they said:]*
- "You mentioned leads going to voicemail after close. We'd build an AI system that responds in under 60 seconds, answers their questions, and either books them or routes them to you."
- "You said the follow-up process is inconsistent. We'd build automated sequences that keep leads warm for 7–14 days without anyone on your team lifting a finger."
- "You mentioned [specific pain] — that's exactly what our [specific feature] handles."

"I don't want to pitch you a package right now — I want to give you something more useful. What I'd like to do is put together a 1-page AI Action Plan specific to [Business Name] and send it to you within 24 hours. That'll give you something concrete to react to."

---

## NEXT STEP CLOSE (Minutes 27–30) — Set Up the Audit

"Here's what I'd suggest as a next step: let me put together your free AI audit — it'll show the 3 specific automation opportunities for [Business Name], an ROI estimate based on the numbers we talked about, and a recommended path forward."

"I'll send that within 24 hours. Then we can hop on a 20-minute call to walk through it, and if a proposal makes sense, we can talk about that then."

"Does that work as a next step?"

*[If yes:]*
"Perfect. And just so I capture this correctly — the best email for the audit is [email they used to book]?"

*[Confirm and close the call.]*

---

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "We're not ready for AI yet."
"That's actually the best time to do the audit — before you're overwhelmed and scrambling. The audit takes 30 minutes and costs nothing. Worst case, you get a clear picture of what AI could do for you when you're ready. What's the risk in knowing?"

### "We already have a website / chatbot."
"That's good news — means less to build from scratch. Most of the businesses we help already have some technology in place; the question is whether it's actually capturing and following up with every lead automatically, or whether things still slip through. What does your current follow-up process look like after someone fills out your form or sends a message?"

### "That's too expensive."
"I hear that. Can I ask — what would make it feel worth it? If I could show you that the system pays for itself in 60–90 days based on your numbers, would it be worth exploring? I'd rather show you the math first than assume it's the wrong fit."

### "I need to think about it."
"Of course — this is a real decision. What would make you feel confident about moving forward? Is it seeing the ROI numbers for your specific situation, or is there something else I haven't addressed?"

### "Can you send me some information?"
"Absolutely. Let me make it specific to your situation — that's what the free AI audit is. Takes me about 24 hours to put together and it'll be more useful than a generic brochure. What email should I send it to?"
