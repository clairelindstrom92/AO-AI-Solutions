# Client Case Study Interview Script — AO AI Solutions
**20-minute guided interview | Record with permission**

---

## BEFORE THE INTERVIEW
- [ ] Ask for permission to record (Zoom, Google Meet, or phone)
- [ ] Have the case study template open and ready
- [ ] Ask if they're comfortable with their name, company, and metrics being shared
- [ ] Schedule for a relaxed time — not right after a stressful day

---

## OPENING (2 min)

"Hi [First Name], thanks so much for taking the time to do this. As I mentioned, I'd love to document what we built together for [Company Name] so we can share it with other [law firms / practices / contractors] facing the same challenges. It'll take about 20 minutes.

I may use a quote or two from our conversation, along with your name and company. Is that okay?

[If yes: great, let's dive in. If no: ask what level of attribution they're comfortable with — could use 'a DC law firm' vs. company name.]

And — is it okay if I record this for my notes? I won't share the recording."

---

## SECTION 1: BEFORE STATE (5 min)
*Goal: Get specific numbers. Quantify the pain.*

"Let's start before we worked together. Tell me about the challenge you were trying to solve. What was actually happening day-to-day that was frustrating you?"

*[Let them talk. Don't interrupt. Take notes.]*

"You mentioned [X] — how much time was your team spending on that per week? Like, if you had to estimate?"

"And what was that costing you in terms of opportunities? Were you losing clients to competitors, or was it more of an internal efficiency issue?"

"If you had to put a number on it — how many inquiries or leads were you losing per month because of [the bottleneck]?"

"What had you already tried before reaching out to us? Did you look at other solutions?"

"How long had this been a problem before we talked?"

---

## SECTION 2: IMPLEMENTATION (3 min)
*Goal: Document what we built. Validate the process experience.*

"When we started working together, what was your first impression of the process? Was it what you expected?"

"What was the deployment experience like? [Were you surprised by how fast it went? Were there any hiccups?]"

"What did we build specifically that made the biggest difference for you?"

---

## SECTION 3: AFTER STATE — RESULTS (7 min)
*Goal: Specific, measurable, attributable numbers.*

"Okay — the results. Let's get specific. What changed after we launched [the AI system]?"

"Do you have actual numbers? [Time saved per week? New clients or bookings per month? Revenue increase? Staff hours reduced?]"

"How long after launch did you start seeing results?"

"What surprised you most about the results? What did you not expect to see?"

"If you had to pick one thing that was the most impactful, what would it be?"

"On a scale of 1–10, how would you rate working with AO AI Solutions?"

---

## SECTION 4: THE QUOTE (3 min)
*Guide them toward a compelling, usable testimonial.*

"I'd love to use a quote from you in the case study. Let me ask it this way — if a [law firm partner / practice owner / contractor] who was skeptical about AI automation asked you directly, 'Is it worth it?' — what would you tell them?"

*[They'll often give you a perfect quote here. Write it down word-for-word.]*

"Can I use that? And how would you like to be attributed — [First Name Last Name, Title, Company Name]?"

---

## SECTION 5: TECHNOLOGIES USED (2 min)
*Confirm what was built so we can list it accurately.*

"Just to confirm for the case study — the system we built for you included:
- [AI chatbot / 24/7 lead capture]
- [Automated follow-up sequences]
- [CRM integration — which CRM?]
- [Booking system integration]
- [Other]

Is that accurate? Is there anything I'm missing?"

---

## CLOSING

"That's everything I needed — this is going to make a great case study. I'll send you a draft to review before we publish anything. Any questions for me?

And just to confirm — you're okay with us sharing:
- Your name: [First Name Last Name]
- Your company: [Company Name]
- The result metrics we discussed
- The quote you gave

Thank you so much, [First Name]. I really appreciate it."
