## HOW TO USE
- Load contacts into HubSpot or Apollo and enroll in sequence
- Replace `[PERSONALIZATION]` with the instruction beneath each email
- Replace `[CALENDLY_LINK]` with: your Calendly booking link
- Send Day 1 on a Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am or 1–3pm local time
