## SCORE THRESHOLDS & ACTIONS

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 70–100 | 🔴 Hot | Personal outreach within 24 hours. Call or video DM. Priority. |
| 50–69 | 🟠 Warm | Add to high-touch follow-up. LinkedIn + email within 48 hours. |
| 30–49 | 🟡 Nurture | Stay in email sequence. Check in monthly. |
| 0–29 | 🔵 Cold | Leave in sequence. No extra manual effort. |

## HOW TO APPLY IN HUBSPOT

**Option 1 — Manual scoring (HubSpot Starter)**
- Add a custom contact property: "Lead Score" (number field)
- Update score manually when contact behavior changes
- Review weekly and update deal stages accordingly

**Option 2 — Automated scoring (HubSpot Professional)**
- Settings → Lead Scoring → Create new scoring model
- Add each behavior from this rubric as a positive or negative attribute
- HubSpot will auto-update the score in real-time

**Option 3 — Apollo.io built-in scoring**
- Apollo tracks email engagement automatically
- Enable "Intent data" in Apollo settings for additional firmographic scoring
- Export high-scored contacts to HubSpot for manual follow-up
